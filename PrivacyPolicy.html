Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic personal information (name, contact details) and delivery information to provide our services. For student workers, we collect availability schedules.

2. How We Use Information

  • Process orders and deliveries
  • Match student workers with delivery opportunities
  • Improve our services
  • Send important service notifications

3. Data Sharing

We only share necessary delivery information with our student workers and partner stores to fulfill orders. We never sell your data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transmission and storage.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Student workers can update their availability preferences anytime.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through our platform or email.

Effective Date: June 2024
