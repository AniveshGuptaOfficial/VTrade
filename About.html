About VTrade

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by university students, CampusCart was born from the frustration of wasting precious study time traveling for basic necessities. We've created a revolutionary delivery system that leverages student networks to bring campus essentials right to your dorm or classroom.

Our Hybrid Model

We combine the reliability of full-time delivery staff with the flexibility of student workers. This unique approach ensures:

  • 24/7 Availability: Full-time workers cover all time slots
  • Peak Efficiency: Student deliveries during high-demand periods
  • Cost Savings: Lower operational costs mean better prices for you

How It Works

  • Smart Matching: Our algorithm connects orders with students already heading your way
  • Hyperlocal Partners: We source directly from campus and nearby stores
  • Fallback System: Automatic reassignment ensures no delivery is delayed
  • Flexible Delivery: Schedule to your free periods or request immediate delivery

Student Opportunities

Our platform empowers students through:

  • Flexible Earnings: Make money between classes
  • Rewards Program: Top deliverers earn bonuses and perks
  • Community Building: Connect with fellow students through deliveries
